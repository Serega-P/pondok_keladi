About Guest House

Pondok Keladi Guest House combines the charm of Traditional Malay design with modern amenities on an acre of land conveniently located in a little village of Pantai Tengah, Langkawi. Nestled at the foot of a hill and surrounded by coconut palm trees, rainforest and overlooking the paddy fields

Pondok Keladi has a unit of six bedrooms complete with open-sided centrally located, covered area called the Balai complete with a kitchen.
